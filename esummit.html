BizQuiz

Just as the name suggests in Business Quiz the participants will be asked a number of questions regarding businesses and start-ups. The quiz will really explore the knowledge of the participants about industries, companies, and products.

Event Details

B-Plan Pitching

All the entrepreneurs who have a business idea need money to make their idea into reality. That is exactly what this competition is all about! Getting the investors to invest in your idea! Participants can come in teams (max limit- 4 people). Teams can bring a presentation if they require. Limit for each team will be a total of 4 minutes on stage and 1 minute off stage. The team which is able to impress our judges the most will be the winners.

Event Details
Date :   1:30pm - 3:30pm

Ad Venture - Ad Making Competition

Advertisements are a very effective tools for companies to market their products in today’s ever increasing competitive markets. And that’s exactly what this competition is all about, making an ad for your product.

The teams can have maximum 5 members and they will be given a product on the spot. The teams will be given ample time to prepare. The ads should be attractive, there should be punchline, it should explain everything about the products, and all other details like price, offers etc. Participants will have to enact their ads on the stage and will be judged accordingly. Maximum time limit will be 5 minutes per team.

FAQS

  • What is Entrepreneurship Summit?

    'Entrepreneurship Summit' is the annual entrepreneurship gathering that convenes emerging entrepreneurs, investors and college students across the country. 'Entrepreneurship Summit 2018' is based on the theme - Upgrading to Upstand, that involves discussions around implementing changes for sustainable growth.

    Broadly, eCell MSIT is organizing 3 types of events under the Summit viz. Conclave, Internship-cum-Job Fair & Competitons. Through two days of networking, presentations, activities, eCell MSIT empowers entrepreneurs and students to pitch their ideas, build partnerships, seek mentorship, innovate, and find their target customers -- creating new goods and services that will transform societies.

  • What will happen in 'Conclave' of the Entrepreneurship Summit?

    'Conclave' is the conference that explores various established professionals from the world of business, venturing and entrepreneurship. We have organised a series of talks and other enlightening sessions in various forms like- Panel Talks, Workshops, Success Stories and Networking Events.

  • What happens in 'Internship-cum-Job Fair' (IJF)?

    IFJ brings various companies and students to a common platform where both communities can help each other in their growth. For companies, it is an opportunity to hire interns or employees from a pool of different types of students in a quick manner. For students, it is an opportunity to connect to the corporate world and use their skills on live projects.

    Before interviews begin, there will be a session where all startups will introduce themselves and their requirements. A student can appear for a limited number of physical interviews only and that will be disclosed on the day of event. However, if a student requests, eCell MSIT can share a digital copy of his/her resume with other companies (subject to availability).

  • How do I buy tickets for the event?

    By clicking on the "BUY TICKET" button, you shall be directed to a registration form. After entering your details, you will be shown the ticket price for the event. Click on "Proceed to Payment" button. Select you payment method and follow the rest of instructions accordingly.

    After successful payment, you shall receive a confirmation message regarding the same. Within 48 Hours, you will receive a unique identity code (UID) for the event through email and SMS. Keep it handy throughout the duration of event.

  • I want to attend IJF/Conclave only and not the complete Summit. Is their any concession?

    No. Buying a ticket makes you eligible to attend all the events in the Entrepreneurship Summit. It's upto you, which events you wish to attend. For clarity, we sugeest you to check out the schedule.

  • I have purchased the ticket but won't be able to attend the Summit. Can my friend use my ticket to attend the Summit?

    No. The tickets are non-tranferable, non-refundable and there is no-cancellation policy.

  • How much stipend/salary will these companies provide to the hired candidates?

    Stipend/Salary, work hours, locations, etc. are decided between the candidate and employer. eCell MSIT is not responsible for any conflicts after the event. It is advisable to have a primary research before joining any company.

  • I lost/found an item at the venue. Whom should I report to?

    Please drop an email to connect@ecellmsit.in mentioning all possible details and we will get back to you if there is any update. eCell MSIT holds no reponsibility for loss/damage to any private property of the attendees.

  • How can I reach to MSIT?

    Here is the link to Google Map for the venue.

    If you are coming via Metro, it is advisable to deboard at Janakpuri East and take 'Gramin Seva', e-rickshaw or normal rickshaw and ask them to drop you at "Maharaja Surajmal College, Gate Number 2".

    If you are coming through other means, these landmarks will help you reach quite quickly - Bharati College, Fire Station (Janakpuri), District Park (Janakpuri), Janakpuri Super Speciality Hospital.

  • What things should I bring to the event?

    1. College Identity Card (Hard Copy, mandatory).
    2. Confirmation of successful registration/UID (mandatory).
    3. Atleast 3 copies of your resume (in case, you are appearing for IJF).
    4. Notepad
    5. Pen
    6. Formal Dress (not mandatory)

  • I have purchased the ticket. Will I be provided with meals or refreshments?

    There will be no meals/refreshments for student attendees. Attendees can access college canteens, food stalls or nearby shops to have meal at their own cost.

  • I can't find my question in these FAQs. What to do?

    Don't worry. Mention your query to connect@ecellmsit.in and we will answer it in earliest possible manner.
